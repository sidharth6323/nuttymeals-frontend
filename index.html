FAQ's
1. Why should you choose us?
We work as per and for the customer. Serving you with the perfect blend of the mixtures and spices at the least possible cost is our forte.
2. What are our operation timings?
We serve breakfast from 7:30 am to 8:30 am our lunch timings are from 12:30 pm to 1:30 pm and, we serve dinner from 7:30 am to 8:30 pm
3. What is the price of our meals? Are there any special offers for office working people or certain deals?
The full meal packages amount to Rs.3999 but can be reduced down to Rs.3799 for 30 days. And for 15 days, the amount is Rs.1999. The office employees can opt for the breakfast and dinner combo meals which actually cost Rs.2999 but they can avail the early bird discount which will reduce the meal cost to Rs.1399.
4. What is the credibility of your visual gallery?
We simply abide by the rule of actually serving what all we portray through images. All our pictures are taken while they are being prepared only with quality spices and ingredients.
5. What are our areas of operation?
Right now we revolve around places in and near Rajareshwari Nagar only. We will be expanding our wings to other places soon.
6. Are there any delivery charges?
No, we do not charge anything extra for the delivery.
7. Will you abide by any changes as per the customer convenience?
Yes. For example, if you cancel the meal, all you need to do is inform us 12 hours before. The amount of the cancelled meal will be forwarded to the next subscription period. If you wish t get the meal delivered at some other place, you should inform us atleast 3 hours before the delivery schedule.
